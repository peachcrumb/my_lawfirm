OUR ATTORNEY
Nicholas J. Zabala, Esq.
Nick is a seasoned attorney with a dynamic background in law, business, and entrepreneurship. With a commitment to excellence and a passion for serving clients, Nick brings invaluable experience and expertise to every case.
Nick graduated from Temple University in 2009, earning a Bachelor of Science in Sport Management. Driven by a desire to explore the intersection of law and business, he continued his academic journey at the Thomas R. Kline School of Law at Drexel University. In 2015, he obtained his Juris Doctor with a concentration in business and entrepreneurship.
With a solid foundation in law, Nick embarked on his professional career at a prestigious midsize law firm, where he spent six years representing financial institutions in foreclosure matters. During this time, he honed his legal skills and developed a deep understanding of the complexities of financial law.
In 2021, Nick transitioned his practice to focus on representing alternative finance companies, bringing his expertise to a new realm of the legal landscape. With his comprehensive knowledge of financial law and a keen understanding of business dynamics, he is well-equipped to navigate the challenges and opportunities facing alternative finance companies in today’s ever-evolving market.
Committed to delivering exceptional results and providing strategic guidance to his clients, Nick is dedicated to upholding the highest standards of professionalism, integrity, and client service. Whether advocating in the courtroom or providing counsel at the negotiation table, he is unwavering in his pursuit of achieving the best possible outcomes for those he represents.
With his unique blend of legal acumen, business savvy, and dedication to client success, Nick is a trusted advocate and advisor for individuals and businesses alike.
